BLRST Restaurant Database



About


The Restarant Database by the BLRST team is intended for use by restaurants as a means for them to be able to track their records.


As seen in the directory to the left, this database can be used to find information regarding the menu items, stock items, staff members, and finances of your restaurant. You may use this directory to navigate to other portions of the site to find this information.

Once navigating to the pages listed in the directory, you will then be able to find a more in depth list of queries that can be made to determine a more detailed analysis on your restaurant's data.


For more information regarding these queries, please navigate to their listing on their respective pages.





Used By


The BLRST Restaurant Database is restricted to usage by only restaurants that have been authorized by the owners.


The database can only be used as intended and cannot be reformatted or redistributed in any form at any point in time without prior consent from the BLRST Co. Any violations will be swiftly met with legal action and will be subject to "questioning" by the SFBI (Schrute Farms Buraeu of Investigation). It is in your best interests to not cross Schrute Farms.

In order to receive authorization, you and your restaurant must pass a rigourous background check to ensure you comply with the BLRST Co. standards. These standards, interviews, and background checks, etc. will be random and scheduled at our behest. To ensure best results for you and your restaurant, we strongly advise you to assume you are already being evaluated to see if you will truly be a good fit.


For more information regarding obtaining an authorized contract, please contact Dwight K. Schrute at Schrute Farms.
